About Pharma Edutech

Pharma Eductech is a well-established organization in the field of Pharmacy Education borne out of deep sense of the concern is dedicated to the cause of quality coaching to shape the young minds to establish themselves in highly competitive academic and career world. We are the No.1 coaching institute for the all Pharmacy PG entrance examinations such as GPAT, NIPER-JEE, BITSPG (for M.Pharmcy) and MANIPALCET (for M.Pharmcy) and also we provide coaching for all pharmacy related government sector jobs like Drugs inspector, Pharmacist, Government Analyst, etc. PHARMA EDUCTECH provides specialized courses which are designed by experts in the respective fields.
